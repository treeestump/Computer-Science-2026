Summary

This program calculates the area and perimeter of a regular polygon. It takes three user inputs: the number of sides, the length of each side, and the apothem (the distance from the center to the midpoint of a side). It then computes the perimeter by multiplying sides by side length, and the area using the formula (perimeter × apothem) / 2.
